Industries We Serve

Optic Bee has deep expertise across multiple industries, providing specialized NetSuite solutions for your unique business challenges.

NetSuite for Manufacturing

Streamline your manufacturing operations with NetSuite's industry-specific features. Our solutions help you manage production, supply chain, inventory, and quality control.

Key benefits:

  • Real-time production monitoring
  • Supply chain management
  • Work order management
  • Quality assurance processes

NetSuite for Retail & E-commerce

Transform your retail business with unified commerce solutions. Manage online and in-store sales, inventory, and customer experience from a single platform.

Key benefits:

  • Unified shopping experience
  • Inventory visibility across channels
  • Customer loyalty management
  • Order and fulfillment optimization

NetSuite for Software & Technology

Manage your software business effectively with NetSuite's specialized solutions for technology companies. Handle complex revenue recognition, subscription billing, and renewals.

Key benefits:

  • Advanced revenue recognition
  • Subscription billing management
  • Project management and accounting
  • SuiteSuccess for Technology

NetSuite for Healthcare

Address the unique needs of healthcare organizations with NetSuite's robust solutions. Manage compliance, patient billing, and operational efficiency.

Key benefits:

  • HIPAA compliant features
  • Patient billing and records
  • Inventory management for medical supplies
  • Financial management

NetSuite for Nonprofit Organizations

Drive your mission forward with NetSuite's nonprofit solutions. Manage donors, grants, programs, and financial accountability in one system.

Key benefits:

  • Donor and grant management
  • Fund accounting
  • Program management
  • Reporting for board and stakeholders
