The CPG Landscape

Tackling Your Toughest Challenges

In an industry of razor-thin margins and relentless change, CPG companies face a perfect storm of hurdles. We provide the tools to turn these challenges into opportunities.

Supply Chain Volatility

From sourcing disruptions to fluctuating logistics costs, managing inventory and demand is more complex than ever. Lack of visibility leads to stockouts or costly overstock.

Intense Margin Pressure

Rising input costs, fierce price competition, and retailer negotiations squeeze profitability. Accurate cost-to-serve and profitability analysis are non-negotiable.

Shifting Consumer Demands

The rise of D2C, private labels, and demand for sustainable products requires agile innovation and marketing. Predicting trends is key to staying relevant.

The Optic Bee Solution

Connected Planning for CPG Excellence

We implement Oracle's EPM Cloud to create a single, intelligent platform that connects your entire organization—from finance and sales to marketing and supply chain.

  • Unified View of Performance: Break down data silos to get a single source of truth for all your planning, forecasting, and reporting needs.
  • AI-Powered Foresight: Leverage predictive analytics and machine learning to create more accurate demand forecasts and run what-if scenarios in real-time.
  • Enhanced Agility: Respond faster to market shifts, competitor actions, and supply chain disruptions with dynamic, driver-based planning capabilities.
Our Oracle EPM Services

A Tailored Approach for the CPG Industry

We offer a full spectrum of Oracle EPM implementation and advisory services, customized for the unique demands of CPG manufacturers and distributors.

Integrated Business Planning & Forecasting

Align strategic goals with operational execution. We help you build a connected planning ecosystem for superior demand forecasting, effective trade promotion management, and agile supply chain response.

  • Statistical and ML-driven demand planning.
  • Trade promotion planning, execution, and ROI analysis.
  • Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP) alignment.
  • Driver-based "what-if" scenario modeling for pricing and volume.

Accelerated Financial Consolidation & Close

Streamline your period-end close from weeks to days. Our solutions automate complex consolidations, intercompany eliminations, and currency conversions, ensuring a fast, accurate, and auditable close process.

  • Automated data loading and validation from source systems.
  • Streamlined intercompany transaction matching and elimination.
  • Comprehensive close process management and dashboards.
  • Ensured data accuracy for internal and external reporting.

Granular Profitability & Cost Management

Uncover the true drivers of your profitability. We build sophisticated allocation models to give you clear visibility into SKU, brand, customer, and channel profitability, enabling smarter portfolio and pricing decisions.

  • Detailed cost-to-serve analysis for every customer.
  • "Pocket margin" analysis to identify profit leakage.
  • Activity-based costing for accurate overhead allocation.
  • Insights to optimize product mix and eliminate unprofitable SKUs.
