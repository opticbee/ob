Optic Bee Foundation

Passionate about giving back to the local communities we serve, Optic Bee Soft Tech established the Optic Bee Foundation because we care.

The Optic Bee Foundation is part of the heart and soul of Optic Bee Soft Tech.

Our Mission

The Optic Bee Foundation donates time, money, and in-kind resources to empower youth and their families. We partner with promising, quality, and caring non-profit organizations who provide direct support to local communities.

We Are Passionate

Committed to empowering youth and families in communities where we have Optic Bee hubs.

We Strive To

Create positive impact, support local organizations, and inspire collaboration toward service opportunities.

Create a Positive and Lasting Impact

Empowering Youth In Our Community

At the Optic Bee Foundation, we understand that helping in a big way doesn't always mean a big event. That's why throughout the year we work with our partners and charity organizations in order to get directly involved in the local community.

Giving Back

Whether that means helping students with STEM projects, organizing tech donation drives, or mentoring aspiring young developers, we're there to help and give!

Promote and Support Local Organizations

Partnering With Local Organizations

We believe the best way forward is always together! The Optic Bee Foundation partners with and helps to support local organizations that align with our mission and values.

Supporting Youth In Our Community

In order to support that vision, each year we partner with local schools and organizations to host a Tech Day for youth. We truly believe that technology can change the life of a child for the better.

Every resource donated goes directly to our partner organizations.
