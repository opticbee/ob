Our Development Journey

Tracing our path from a visionary idea to a portfolio of impactful digital products that redefine industries.

2018: The Genesis

Our journey began with the launch of Hitaishi Financials, aiming to democratize investment tools for everyone.

2019: Entering E-Commerce

We launched Gadicart, our first multi-vendor marketplace, empowering small businesses to sell online.

2020: Focus on Health

Hitaishi Healthcare was developed to digitize hospital operations and improve patient outcomes during a critical time.

2021: Connecting People

Launched Winjob to tackle unemployment challenges and introduced 4Jodi, a modern matrimony platform.

2022: Expanding Mobility

GoRiders hit the streets, offering a smart, safe, and efficient urban transportation solution.

2023: The Future is Now

Innovating with AI in all our platforms and launching our first major Sports engagement app.
